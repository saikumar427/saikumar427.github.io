About Us

Sevenverge is a design firm based out of Hyderabad, india. Our work is based on the specific and unique conditions of each project, on its context and on the aspirations of the client. We work around lines and volumes to create spaces. We love to experiment with materials. White is the basis of all our projects. We bring the colors in touch to reveal certain details by working around contrasts between dark and light shades, warm and cold.

“Architecture is a means of providing solutions to make everyday life easier, but also a little poetry in everyday life. Beauty is often in the simplest details” - Principal Architect

Welcome To Our Company

Seven Verge is a design practice established for the intersection details of art, architecture and design. The architect is experienced in designing, managing projects and designs a space that integrates the client’s personality and life experience, and interprets the connotation and the story of the space. The architect is registered with the Council of Architecture - India.

I believe that design is not only about creating a good space, elegant buildings or houses. It’s about creating a better place to live because everyone has their own dreams to live, every design should be personal and authentic

Shruthi Gajawada Principal Architect

Company Story

Seven Verge is a privately owned practice, dedicated to innovative, practical design solutions. The Sevenverge’s name is inspired by the principles of design and elements of art. We see architecture as how we architects solve a problem beautifully. For us, architecture is a blend between art and engineering to create harmony of spaces for a better living. Each project, whether large or small, is considered unique and design solutions suit the individual requirements of the client and location. All projects are managed and administrated from the concept design stage to final handover, resulting in successfully completed, quality output.
